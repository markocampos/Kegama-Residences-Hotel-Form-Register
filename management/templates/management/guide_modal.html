Usage Guide: Guest Details

1. Guest Profile

Update personal details like name, contact info, and booking source. Ensure the Phone Number follows the 09XX XXX XXXX format (auto-formatted).

2. Room & Duration

  • Room Selection: Choose a room to auto-fill the standard rate and capacity.
  • Check-in/Out: Manually pick dates OR use the Duration Buttons (6H, 10H, 22H) for quick setting.
  • Short Stays: Setting "Nights" to 0 will label the stay as "Short Stay" on the PDF.
  • Dates & Nights: Changing dates automatically calculates nights, and vice-versa.

3. Financials

  • Room Rate: Can be manually overridden. Use numbers only.
  • Mode of Payment: Select from CASH, GCASH, MAYA, or BANK TRANSFER.
  • Additional Requests: Click "ADD ITEM" to include extra charges (e.g., Extra Bed, Food). These appear on the receipt.

4. Actions

  • Update Details Only: Saves changes to the database without generating a file.
  • Confirm & Print PDF: Saves changes AND downloads the official Guest Registration Form PDF.
